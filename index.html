Let's set up your Macbook password!

At this point, you should be logged in to VPN & OKTA They're pre-requisites for below steps.
  • Click on the jamf Icon on top right corner
  • Select the change password option from the list
  • Enter the old and new password to complete change
  • Done

Your new password must have at least 10 characters including:
  • 1 upper case
  • 1 lower case and
  • 1 number
  • Most importantly, it must not include any variation of your name.

    • Let's set your windows laptop password

    • While on the Windows desktop screen, press CTRL + ALT + DEL on the keyboard.
    • Choose the "Change a password" option and
    • create your new password following the rules below.
    Your new password must have at least 10 characters including:
  • 1 upper case
  • 1 lower case and
  • 1 number
  • Most importantly, it must not include any variation of your name.

    • The old password is the initial password that you used to login the first time.

    Creat a new Bitlocker pin

    • Click on Windows Start button and search for the word "bitlocker"
    • On the "bitlocker drive encryption page" select the "change pin" option.
    • Create your new pin.
      You can use the same one as your newly made Windows password and
    • Click on "Change PIN".
    Your new pin must have at least 10 characters including:
  • 1 upper case
  • 1 lower case and
  • 1 number
  • Most importantly, it must not include any variation of your name.

    • The Bitlocker pin should be the same password you set up for your Windows account.

    Connect to VPN

    • Did you notice the QR code in the email you received with your account and initial password? So, that is your Wise VPN token.
      Open the Google Authenticator app on your phone and scan that QR code. This would add a token named "TransferWise VPN"
    • Now, search and open the'Cisco AnyConnect' app on your laptop. This might already be pinned to your dock.
    • Fill out the blank field with the address "vpn.transferwise.com" (or vpn.wise.com) and click 'Connect'.
    • Next, enter your Username and Password: the same ones sent to your email, and then click 'Ok'.
    • The next prompt should ask for an "Answer". Please fill this with the token code from the Google Authenticator app on your phone.
    By the end of this set up, you should have two different token numbers in your Google Authenticator app.
    One for Okta and the other for VPN. Make sure to always use the correct one!

    Join Wise slack workspace

    • Click to launch your Okta apps
    • Click on the Wise slack app
    • This should launch slack in your browser

    Log in to your Wise Email

    • Open the Google Chrome browser and click to open the okta sign-in page
    • Enter your username and password: same ones sent to your email
    • Next, scroll down to "google authenticator" from the list and click "setup".
      Note: At this point, please download google authenticator to your or .
      - Next, choose your phone type, click "Next" then a QR code will show up
      - Now turn to your google authenticator app, tap “Get started” or the plus sign somewhere on the screen
      - Tap, “Scan a QR code” to scan the code on your laptop screen
      - Now, enter one of the codes from your google authenticator, click "verify" and "finish"
    • This should take you to your okta dashboard where you'll find the "gmail suite mail" icon.
    • Click on it, and it may take you to the "verify that it's you" page
      - If it does, click "continue" and you should arrive in your Wise gmail inbox.
      - If not, then you should already be in your Wise gmail inbox.
    Okta is responsible for the connection to your Google account and many other services in Wise.

    If you want, you can have more than one 2fa set up for you Okta account, like Google Authenticator and Okta Verify, but at least one is mandatory to keep your access safe!

    Headset set up (For CS teams)

    • Plug the microphone into the female microphone jack on the ear cup and push until the microphone sits flush
    • Connect the detachable headset cable, by plugging the headset part into the female jack on the left headset ear cup
    • Then plug the other end of the detachable headset cable to your laptop.
    • Done

    Make sure to check the control box to adjust the volume and unmute the microphone during use.

    Let's sync up chrome with your Wise email

    1. Click on the profile icon on top right corner.
    2. Click on "Turn on sync".
    3. If it asks for your Wise email, then follow the steps below, otherwise skip to step 8.
    4. Then click Next and enter your Okta credentials.
      username firstname.lastname@transferwise.com
      password: same as your laptop password
    5. Click Sign in. This should take you to the Okta page.
    6. On the okta page, it may request you to enter the okta auth code from google Authenticator
    7. Click continue on the "Verify that it's you" page.
    8. Finally, click yes, I'm in.

    Jamf login and okta 2fa set up

    • Click on the Jamf icon on the top right corner of your screen and select the "Connect" option.

      • Note: At this point, you should be connected to VPN as per the previous set up task 'Connect to VPN'
      If not, please complete that task first.

    • Next, on the jamf connect dialogue box, enter your credentials (if not already filled out)
    • Select the browser type as "chrome" and click ‘Sign In'

      • username: firstname.lastname@transferwise.com
      password: same one sent to email.

    • Enter an okta code to authenticate, click "OK" and you should be on your okta apps page
      Note: You should already have your okta codes setup on google authenticator
      few moments after you powered on your mac.

    At this point, you should have two different token codes on your Google Authenticator app, one for okta and the other for VPN, make sure you are using the correct one!

    Mouse Set up

    First of all, M720 mouse don't need a dongle to connect. They connect via bluetooth

    • Open bluetooth settings from icon on the task bar
    • Select the "add a device" option
    • On the "add a device" page, click on the plus sign and select the first option (bluetooth)
    • Select the "add a device" option
    • Next, press and hold down a number on the mouse till it starts to blink very fast
    • The mouse name "M720 Triathlon" should then appear on the device list (on your screen)
    • Select it and click on Done.
